candidate_selection.py tuning notes

Current workflow profile from nausicaa.py

- Weight sweep samples: 40
- Selected sweep rows / rerun top-k: 5
- Rerun starts: 8
- Rerun keep-top-k: 3
- Note: in the current workflow, the candidate-selection analysis reads these counts
  from the exported workbooks. It does not hard-code them.
