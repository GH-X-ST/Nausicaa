- Primitive: short feedback-stabilised manoeuvre with entry checks, finite horizon, exit checks, metrics, and failure labels.
- Environment context: local flow and safety features computed from the current state and wind field.
- Episode: one simulated or physical launch from an approved launch protocol; launch setup is not an online archive branch.
- W0: dry-air zero-wind validation layer only.
- W1: Gaussian plume validation layer only.
- W2: GP-corrected annular-Gaussian validation layer only.
- W3: randomised GP-corrected annular-Gaussian validation layer only.
- Real flight: approved repeated-launch validation with Vicon-measured initial states.

Global execution rules
